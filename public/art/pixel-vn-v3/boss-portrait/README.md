# Female Professor Boss Battle Portraits

This directory is reserved for the vertical female professor Boss battle
portraits used during the playable Boss fight.

Current source sheet:

- `boss2.png`: user-provided 3x2 female professor contact sheet.

Generated battle portraits are cropped inside the sheet gutters, resized to
`512x960`, and keep the full character framing. They intentionally do not use
blurred side fills.

Expected files:

- `normal.webp`
- `waiting.webp`
- `worried.webp`
- `angry.webp`
- `reactionCloseup.webp`
- `satisfied.webp`

The horizontal files in `../boss/` are only for the finale theater animation.
Do not use old male-professor assets and do not crop or expand horizontal
finale frames into this directory.
